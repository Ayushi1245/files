ye dila divana hai, dila to divana hai
divana dila hai ye dila divana

kaisa bedardi hai, isaki to marzi hai
jaba taka javani hai, ye ruta suhani hai
nazarem juda na hom, aramam khafa na hom
dilakasha baharom mem, chupake chanarom mem
yum hi sada hama tuma, baithe rahem gumasuma
vo bevafa jo kahe hamako jana hai ye dila, 
ye dila divana hai

bechaina rahata hai, chupake se kahata hai
mujhako dha.dakane do, shola bha.dakane do
ka.ntom mem kaliyom mem, sajana ki galiyom mem
fera lagane do, cho.do bhi jane do
kho to na jaumga, maim lauta aumga
dekha sune samajhe achcha bahana hai ye dila, 
ye dila divana hai

savana ke ate hi, badala ke chate hi
phulom ke mausama mem, phulom ke mausama mem
chalate hi puravai, milate hi tanhai
ulajhake batom mem, kahata hai ratom mem
yadom mem kho jaum, jaldi se so jaum
kyomke sa.nvariya ko sapanom mem ana hai ye dila, 
ye dila divana hai
